Visit Sonoma

...much to do in the Valley of the Moon

Wine and Dine

A selection of local flavors, including causal and higher end dining options. Sonoma offers a vast array of restaurants and of course winieries. Everything from local dives serving delicous homestyle meals to elevated Californian fare. Sonoma Valley has many wineries and tasting rooms featuring Pinot Noir, Cabernet Savignon and many more. Photo credit: Teri Appleton

Accomodations

From the Fairmont to small, quaint inn's. Sonoma features a variety of accomodations. From lush and lavish to more budget friendly rooms. Somona is refreshingly free of cookie cutter hotel chains. Sonoma accomodations are unique and usually locally owned. Photo credit: Fairmont Hotel

Sightseeing

Explore Sonoma and Sonoma Valley! In town, check out places like Sonoma Plaza, featuring many shopping and dining options or visit historical site like Sonoma Historic State Park, the Sonoma Barracks, and General Valejo's Home. Families with Children will love Sonoma's Train. Take a drive up Sonoma Valley through Glen Ellen and Kenwood for more wineries and gorgeous State parks. Photo credit: Teri Appleton
